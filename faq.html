PURCHASING

Q: How do I use a promo code?

A: Add your items to the cart and then press checkout. You will see an "Add promotion code" button.

Q: Why am I waiting in line to checkout?

A: Due to high traffic, we only let a certain amount of people checkout at once. You will be let through within 50 seconds guaranteed.

Q: Can I get a discount?

A: If you are looking to buy more than 4 items, send us an email for a coupon!

Q: Is my payment safe?

A: All payment processing is handled by Stripe. Stripe uses AES-256, an industry-leading encryption standard, to secure its transaction communications.

SHIPPING & HANDLING

Q: How long does it take to make my card?

A: We make the cards within 1 day of purchase.

Q: When will I get my order?

A: We ship all orders through USPS First Class Mail. Expected delivery is about 5-8 days, depending on where you live.

Q: Why is my tracking number not working?

A: The tracking number can only be used with a business USPS account. I can track it for you!

Q: Do you ship internationally?

A: We ship anywhere in the world for free. Delivery times will be longer, and tracking details may be limited.

DESIGNS

Q: What design restrictions are there?

A: We have the right to refuse any request. Innapropriate or offensive designs will be rejected. We will NOT manufacture any cards that are intended to be used for illegal activities. All cards are for entertainment purposes only.

Q: Who creates the designs?

A: All designs are created, or re-created by our graphic design artist. This ensures high quality prints.
